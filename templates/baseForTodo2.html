Expense Tracker

The tracker enables Users can track their expenses and keep an eye on their spending patterns. Users can enter their income and expenses, set budgets, and get notifications when their spending goes over their allotted amount. The feature allows the user to add or edit the expense, also presents the expenses by graph, and displays the history of the user.
